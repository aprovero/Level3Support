PV Insulation Testing Safety & Acceptance Criteria
This form records and aggregates insulation resistance (Megger) test results. Minimum acceptable insulation baselines must follow approved project specifications, local codes, applicable IEC 62446-1 standards, and manufacturer manuals. High voltage testing is extremely hazardous; follow site LOTO and HSE safety protocols.

Standards & Acceptance Rules
  • Under IEC 62446-1, for systems up to 1000V DC, test voltage is 1000VDC, minimum resistance is 1.0 MΩ (or 0.5 MΩ for systems up to 500VDC). For 1500V DC systems, minimum resistance is typically 1.0 MΩ (some project procedures dictate higher limits like 5.0 MΩ).
  • Pass rule: A circuit passes if R+, R-, and R+- (if entered) are all greater than or equal to the minimum required resistance threshold.
  • Review rule: High humidity (>70% or >80%) can degrade surface resistance, producing false fails. Technicians should verify terminals are dry and clean.
