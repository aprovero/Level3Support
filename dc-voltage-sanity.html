Possible Issue Hints
Engineering Assumptions
  • Expected Voltage = Vbase × Nseries × [1 + (TC%/°C ÷ 100) × (Tmodule − 25)]
  • Temperature coefficient entered as %/°C is automatically converted to decimal per °C.
  • STC reference temperature is 25°C.
  • Acceptable range is ±2.5% of expected. Status WARN if measured within ±10%; FAIL outside ±10%.
  • Issue hints are informational only. Always verify with additional measurements and approved procedures.
  • Vmp under field conditions may differ significantly from Vmp at STC due to loading and partial shading effects.
