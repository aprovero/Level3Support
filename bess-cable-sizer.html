Sizing Logic & Formulas
  • Required Design Current = Load Current $\times$ Continuous Factor.
  • Corrected Conductor Ampacity = Base Ampacity $\times$ Temp Factor $\times$ Bundling Factor $\times$ Additional factor.
  • DC & 1-Phase AC Voltage Drop: $VD = 2 \times I \times R \times L$.
  • 3-Phase AC Voltage Drop: $VD = \sqrt{3} \times I \times R \times L$.
  • Power Loss: $P_{loss} = I^2 \times R_{total}$ (Single-phase / DC: $R_{total}$ for 2 conductors; Three-phase AC: $P_{loss} = 3 \times I^2 \times R_{single\_conductor}$).
  • User must enter appropriate conductor resistance per length reflecting operating temperature (e.g. 75°C or 90°C).
