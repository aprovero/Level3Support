Timeline Analysis Guide
  • Cascade Delta: The time interval calculated represents the duration elapsed between that specific row and the preceding event in chronological order.
  • Chatter Detection: Highlighted repeated event codes within millisecond ranges are typically telemetry loop anomalies or mechanical switch bounces.
  • Timezones: Keep timezone labels consistent (e.g. UTC) to prevent shift errors when combining server logs.
