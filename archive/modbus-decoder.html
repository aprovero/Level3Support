MODBUS Bit-Ordering Reference
  • Big Endian Word (ABCD): High word comes first, low word second.
  • Little Endian Word (CDAB): Low word comes first, high word second (common in many solar inverters).
  • 32-Bit Float: Follows the IEEE-754 standard for single precision floating point numbers.
  • Bit 0: Represents the Least Significant Bit (LSB) on the far right of the bitmask.
