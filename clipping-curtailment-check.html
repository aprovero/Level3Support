Diagnostic Assumptions & Limitations
  • Consistent with Clipping is flagged if AC output is >97% of rated AC power and available DC power is higher than AC output.
  • Consistent with Curtailment is flagged if AC output is within 2% of the PPC Active Power limit, or curtailment is selected as active.
  • Possible Underperformance is flagged if available DC is high (>80% of rated) or irradiance is high (>400 W/m²), but AC output is low (<75% of rated) without any limits or curtailments active.
  • This tool does not confirm root cause. Final diagnosis must consider alarms, grid commands, tracker position, and high-speed site SCADA data.
