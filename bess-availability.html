Engineering Assumptions
  • Simple Availability is computed as: (Total Hours - Counted Downtime) / Total Hours × 100.
  • Counted Downtime includes forced (FOH) and planned (POH) outages.
  • Exclusion-Adjusted Availability accounts for exclusions (EOH): (Total Hours - Counted Downtime) / (Total Hours - EOH) × 100.
  • Equivalent Derated Outage Hours computes derates as fractional outages: Derated Hours × (1 - Derated Capacity % / 100).
  • Total Equivalent Outage Hours combines Outages + Derated fractional outages.
  • Status boundaries: Availability ≥ 98.0% is Normal, 95.0% ≤ Avail < 98.0% is Watch, Avail < 95.0% is Critical.
