Safety Rules & Standards Reference
  • PPE Category 1 requires arc-rated clothing of min **4 cal/cm²**.
  • PPE Category 2 requires arc-rated clothing of min **8 cal/cm²** along with arc-rated face shield or hood.
  • PPE Category 3 requires arc-rated hood system of min **25 cal/cm²**.
  • PPE Category 4 requires arc-rated hood system of min **40 cal/cm²**.
  • Any incident energy **>= 40 cal/cm²** is extremely dangerous; energized maintenance work is strictly prohibited under standard safety protocols.
