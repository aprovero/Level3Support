Engineering Assumptions
  • DC / Single-phase formula: VDrop = 2 × I × Rtotal × L.
  • Three-phase AC simple formula: VDrop = √3 × I × Rtotal × L.
  • Three-phase AC advanced formula (uses PF & Reactance): VDrop = √3 × I × L × (R cosφ + X sinφ).
  • sinφ is automatically calculated as: √(1 - cos2φ).
  • Pass criteria is determined if VDrop % ≤ Allowed Voltage Drop %.
