Engineering Assumptions
  • Correction formula: PExpected = PSTC × (GMeas / GRef) × [1 + (γ / 100) × (TMod - TRef)].
  • Temperature coefficient (γ) is converted to a decimal inside the code: γ / 100.
  • Pass/Normal status represents: Measured Power ≥ 90% of Weather-Corrected Expected Power.
  • Watch status represents: 85% ≤ Performance < 90%. Investigate represents: Performance < 85%.
