Engineering Assumptions & Notes
  • All measurements are recorded as measured in the field. No automatic correction to STC (1000 W/m², 25°C) is applied.
  • Irradiance and module temperature are recorded for reference. STC correction requires additional calculation if needed.
  • Curve status classification (Pass / Warning / Fail) is entered by the engineer based on tracer output and visual inspection.
  • Defect types are descriptive categories. Definitive defect diagnosis requires expert review and may require additional testing.
  • Photo attachment support is planned for a future release.
