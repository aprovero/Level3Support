Technical Assumptions & Formulas
  • Duration is calculated as: End Date/Time - Start Date/Time.
  • Delivered Energy: If the user provides a measured energy value, it is used directly. Otherwise, it defaults to: Average Test Power × Duration (Hours).
  • Energy Capacity Performance Ratio: (Delivered Energy / Rated Energy Capacity) × 100.
  • Round-Trip Efficiency (RTE): (Discharged Energy / Charged Energy) × 100. Only calculated when both values are supplied.
  • Capacity calculations are cross-referenced with IEEE 2813 / IEC 62933 energy storage testing frameworks.
