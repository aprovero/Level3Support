Sizing & Tolerance Reference
  • Average Voltage = $\sum(V_n) / N$
  • Voltage Spread = $V_{max} - V_{min}$ (expressed in Volts and Millivolts).
  • Pass/Fail rules: A cell is flagged as **WATCH** if its deviation or total spread exceeds the warning threshold. A cell is flagged as **INVESTIGATE** if its deviation or total spread exceeds the critical threshold.
  • Common values: LFP cells typically balance within **10 - 20 mV** at float charge. Imbalance of **>50 mV** is often flagged as an active alarm in modern BMS architectures.
