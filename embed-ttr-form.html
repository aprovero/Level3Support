TTR Acceptance Limits & Testing Safety
Transformer turns ratio (TTR) limits are typically defined by international standards (e.g. IEEE C57.12.90) and must not exceed **±0.5%** of the expected nominal turns ratio. Verify results against transformer nameplate specifications, manufacturer design basis documents, and site testing safety protocols.

Sizing & Ratio Rules
  • Nominal Ratio = Primary Voltage / Secondary Voltage (For detailed star-delta connections, phase coefficients apply).
  • Ratio Deviation Error % = [(Measured - Expected) / Expected] × 100.
  • Absolute Deviation: |Error %| ≤ Allowed Error % evaluated for PASS/FAIL.
  • Missing calibration dates will generate system warning flags on official printable test logs.
