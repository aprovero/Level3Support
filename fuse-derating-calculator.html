Field Summary
Engineering Assumptions & Limitations
  • This calculator checks current derating only. It does not replace a full protection coordination study.
  • It does not verify: short-circuit protection, interrupting rating, coordination, voltage rating, time-current curves, cable protection, fuse holder rating, or enclosure heating.
  • Temperature derating factors must come from the actual fuse manufacturer datasheet for the specific fuse type and installation conditions.
  • The continuous-current factor (default 1.25) is user-editable and must match the applicable project design basis.
  • Final fuse selection must follow: approved project documents, local electrical code, fuse manufacturer data, equipment manuals, and protection coordination studies.
  • The generic fuse size list is for selection reference only. Not all ratings are available for every fuse type, voltage rating, or manufacturer.
  • No NEC, IEC, UL, OEM, or project compliance is claimed by this tool.
