Engineering QA/QC Verification Standards
  • Scale Check: Ensure Analog points match field readings within allowed sensor tolerances and linear scaling values (e.g. 4-20mA scaling).
  • Time Check: Timestamps must be synchronized within ±1 second across device clocks, RTUs, and the master SCADA server (using NTP/IRIG-B).
  • Hist Check: Confirm tags are correctly logged in the historian database (OPC DA/UA or Modbus poll loops) at regular intervals.
  • Ctrl Check: Digital commands and Setpoints must verify physical feedback loops and emergency override locks.
