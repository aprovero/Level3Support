Engineering Assumptions & Boundaries
  • Delta-T is calculated as: TReturn - TSupply.
  • Cooling Mode Normal Delta-T: 8 to 12 °C (15 to 22 °F) is standard.
  • Cooling Mode Low Capacity Warning: Delta-T < 8 °C (15 °F) indicates weak compressor charge or clogged air filters.
  • Cooling Mode Critical Error: Delta-T ≤ 0 °C (0 °F) indicates complete failure (supplying heat or compressor bypass).
  • Heating Mode Normal Delta-T: 10 to 20 °C (18 to 36 °F) return-supply difference (supply should be warmer than return, giving a negative Delta-T in this standard formula).
