Derating Analysis Assumptions & Limitations
  • Thermal Derating is flagged if internal IGBT temperature is high (≥ 85°C) or ambient is high (≥ 40°C).
  • Grid Voltage/Frequency Derating occurs if grid frequency is outside nominal range (e.g. ≥ 60.05 Hz for overfrequency power curtailment or voltage is ≥ 108% of nominal).
  • Reactive Power / Power Factor Limitation happens if PF is ≤ 0.95, forcing the inverter to dedicate power capacity to kvar instead of active kW.
  • This tool assists in screening potential causes but is an estimation aid. Actual inverter firmware logic, temperature sensors, grid ride-through setups, and SCADA commands override these generic rules.
