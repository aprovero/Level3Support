Cross-Check Assumptions & Limitations
  • Formula: Mismatch % = abs(Sensor A - Sensor B) / Average(Sensor A, Sensor B) × 100.
  • Mismatch ≥ 5% triggers a "Watch" status; mismatch ≥ 10% triggers a "Review Required" status.
  • Dirty sensors and long time intervals since cleaning increase risk of drift or false low readings.
  • This tool assists in identifying potential sensor soiling, dirty reference cells, or severe tilt misalignment but does not replace formal ISO 9060/IEC 61724 calibration protocols.
