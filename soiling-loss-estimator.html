Engineering Assumptions & Limitations
  • This tool offers a localized estimate. Contractual soiling analysis must follow approved project site methodologies.
  • Clean and soiled measurements must be captured under matching ambient irradiance, temperature, operational state, and time conditions.
  • Results are invalid if the compared equipment is affected by clipping, active curtailment, inverter limiters, tracker misalignments, shade, faults, or electrical malfunctions.
