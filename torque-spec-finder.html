Engineering Reference Information
  • Standard Lug Lube Factor (K): Torque is heavily dependent on lubrication. Unlubricated threads require higher torque values compared to lubricated ones. Always check if specs specify dry or greased threads.
  • Copper vs Aluminum Terminals: Aluminum lugs suffer from cold flow/creep. Always verify matching torque settings and anti-oxidant joint compound requirements.
  • Data listed as "Sample only" consists of common industry estimates (e.g. common busbar connections) and must be replaced by project-specific datasheets.
