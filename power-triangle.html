Formulas & Definitions
  • Apparent Power: S = √(P² + Q²).
  • Power Factor: PF = P / S = cos(φ).
  • Phase Angle: φ = arccos(PF) in degrees.
  • Reactive Power: Q = P × tan(φ).
  • 3-Phase Line Current: I = S_VA / (√3 × V_LL).
