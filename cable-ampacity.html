Important Sizing Limitation Warning
Final cable sizing must be checked against the applicable national or local electrical codes (e.g. NEC Article 310, IEC 60364) and site-specific project documents. Ambient temperature correction calculations herein reflect standard NEC tables for 30 °C reference baselines.
Engineering Assumptions
  • Base Reference Ambient temperature is 30 °C.
  • Automatic temperature derating factor matches standard NEC Table 310.15(B)(1) [or equivalent Table 310.16 correction tables].
  • Automatic Conductor adjustment factors: 1-3 conductors = 1.0, 4-6 = 0.80, 7-9 = 0.70, 10-20 = 0.50, 21-30 = 0.45, 31-40 = 0.40, >40 = 0.35.
  • Pass threshold requires: Design Load Current ≤ Corrected Ampacity.
